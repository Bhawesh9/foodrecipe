Samosa Recipe

Ingredients:

  • For the dough:
    • 2 cups all-purpose flour
    • 1/4 cup oil or ghee
    • 1/2 teaspoon salt
    • Water, as needed
  • For the filling:
    • 2 cups boiled and diced potatoes
    • 1/2 cup boiled and chopped mixed vegetables (peas, carrots, corn)
    • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
    • 1 teaspoon ginger paste
    • 1 teaspoon garam masala
    • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
    • 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder
    • Salt to taste

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Dough:
    • In a bowl, mix the all-purpose flour, salt, and oil or ghee.
    • Add water gradually and knead to form a stiff dough. Cover and let it rest for 30 minutes.
  2. Prepare the Filling:
    • In a pan, heat oil and add cumin seeds.
    • Add ginger paste and saut√© for a minute.
    • Add boiled potatoes, mixed vegetables, garam masala, turmeric powder, red chili powder, and salt. Mix well and cook for a few minutes.
