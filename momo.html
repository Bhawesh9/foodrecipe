Momo Recipe

Ingredients:

  • For the dough:
    • 2 cups all-purpose flour
    • Water, as needed
    • Salt to taste
  • For the filling:
    • 1 pound ground meat (chicken, pork, or beef)
    • 1 cup finely chopped cabbage
    • 1/2 cup finely chopped onions
    • 2 cloves garlic, minced
    • 1 teaspoon ginger paste
    • 1 teaspoon soy sauce
    • 1 teaspoon oil
    • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Dough:
    • In a bowl, combine the all-purpose flour and salt.
    • Add water gradually and knead to form a smooth and pliable dough. Cover and let it rest for 30 minutes.
  2. Prepare the Filling:
    • In a pan, heat oil and saut√© garlic and ginger until fragrant.
    • Add chopped onions and cook until translucent.
    • Add ground meat and cook until it's no longer pink.
    • Stir in chopped cabbage, soy sauce, salt, and pepper. Cook until the cabbage is tender.
