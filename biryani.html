Chicken Biryani Recipe

Ingredients:

  • For the chicken marinade:
    • 1 pound (450g) chicken, cut into pieces
    • 1 cup plain yogurt
    • 1 tablespoon ginger paste
    • 1 tablespoon garlic paste
    • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
    • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
    • Salt to taste
  • For the rice:
    • 2 cups basmati rice, soaked for 30 minutes
    • 4-5 cups water
    • 1 bay leaf
    • 4-5 whole cloves
    • 4-5 green cardamom pods
    • 1 cinnamon stick
    • Salt to taste

Instructions:

  1. Marinate the Chicken:
    • In a bowl, mix the chicken with yogurt, ginger paste, garlic paste, red chili powder, turmeric powder, and salt. Let it marinate for at least 1 hour.
