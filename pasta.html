Pasta Recipe

Ingredients:

  • For the pasta:
    • 8 ounces (225g) pasta (e.g., spaghetti, penne, fettuccine)
    • Salt for pasta water
  • For the sauce:
    • 2 tablespoons olive oil
    • 2 cloves garlic, minced
    • 1 can (14 oz / 400g) crushed tomatoes
    • 1 teaspoon dried basil
    • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
    • Salt and pepper to taste
    • Red pepper flakes (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Cook the Pasta:
    • Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil.
    • Add the pasta and cook according to package instructions until al dente.
    • Drain the pasta and set aside.
  2. Prepare the Sauce:
    • In a skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat.
    • Add minced garlic and saut√© for about 1 minute until fragrant.
    • Add crushed tomatoes, dried basil, dried oregano, salt, pepper, and red pepper flakes if using.
    • Simmer the sauce for about 15-20 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  3. Combine Pasta and Sauce:
    • Add the cooked pasta to the skillet with the sauce.
    • Toss the pasta in the sauce until well coated.
    • Cook for an additional 2-3 minutes until the pasta is heated through.
  4. Serve:
    • Divide the pasta into serving plates.
    • Top with grated cheese and fresh herbs if desired.
