Classic Homemade Burger Recipe

Ingredients:

  • For the patties:
    • 1 pound (450g) ground beef (80% lean)
    • Salt and pepper to taste
    • Optional seasonings: garlic powder, onion powder, paprika
  • For assembling:
    • 4 burger buns
    • Lettuce leaves
    • Sliced tomatoes
    • Sliced onions
    • Cheese slices (cheddar, American, or your preference)
    • Condiments (ketchup, mustard, mayonnaise)
    • Pickles (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Patties:
    • In a mixing bowl, add the ground beef.
    • Season with salt, pepper, and any optional seasonings you prefer. Mix gently with your hands, being careful not to overwork the meat.
    • Divide the meat into 4 equal portions and shape them into burger patties. Make the edges slightly thicker than the center to ensure even cooking.
